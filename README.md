## Features

- **Multiple unit categories** — Length, Currency, Mass, Volume, Temperature, and more
- **Instant conversion** — results update in real time as you type
- **Swap units** — quickly reverse the conversion direction with a single tap
- **Saved Conversions** — pin your favourite conversion pairs (e.g. EUR → USD, m → ft) for one-tap access
- **History** — review all your recent conversions at a glance, with timestamps
- **Copy Result** — copy the converted value to clipboard with one tap
- **Dark & Light mode** — toggle between themes from the top bar
- **Search** — quickly find any unit group via the search bar

## How to Use

1. Select a **unit category** from the tab bar (Length, Currency, Mass, Volume…) or use the **search bar**.
2. Enter a value in the **FROM** field and choose your source unit.
3. The converted result appears instantly in the **TO** field. Choose your target unit with the dropdown.
4. Tap the **swap button** (↑↓) to reverse the conversion direction.
5. Tap **Copy Result** to copy the value to your clipboard.
6. Tap the **bookmark icon** to save the conversion pair for quick access later.
7. Tap the **history button** (bottom-right) to review past conversions.
